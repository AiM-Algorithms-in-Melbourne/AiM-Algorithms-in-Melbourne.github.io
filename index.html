About

The AiM (Algorithms in Melbourne) Group is a research group initiated by Dr. Junhao Gan, Dr. William Umboh, and Dr. Hanzhi Wang, based at the School of Computing and Information Systems (CIS), The University of Melbourne (UoM), Australia. The group’s primary research focus is on algorithms and data structures.

We regularly announce upcoming seminars, vacant PhD/PostDoc positions, visiting opportunities, and summaries of previous seminar recordings here.
